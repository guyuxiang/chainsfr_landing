Your questions answered

Is Chainsfer free?
Yes. The Chainsfer service itself does not charge any service fee. However you still have to pay transaction fees on the blockchain. The average fee per transaction is listed below:
  • ~ 0.04 USD for ETH, DAI and other ERC-20 tokens
  • ~ 1 USD for BTC
How does Chainsfer work and how safe is Chainsfer?
As a financial and cryptocurrency service, we try our best to build Chainsfer as secure as possible. Chainsfer service use your Google account to store your crypto wallet and transfer information.
How long does a transfer take?
A single transfer involves two cryptocurrency transactions, one for sending and one for receiving. Chainsfer automatically chooses the median of transaction fees from the last several blocks, assuming a typical daily transaction volume, the average time it takes to complete one transaction is ~ 15 seconds for sending out ETH, DAI and other ERC20 tokens, 15 seconds for receiving ~ 60 minutes for sending out BTC, and another 60 minutes for receiving
What if I enter my recipient's email wrong?
We encrypt your transfer data with the security answer provided. Only the one having the correct security answer and the corresponding transfer email link is able to receive the funds. The transfer expires after 30 days if not claimed by the recipient. To cancel the transfer, see Cancellation Section for details.
