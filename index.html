How to Send

3
Review and Send

We Support

Drive Wallet
Drive Wallet is a Google-powered Non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet. It abstracts out private key management to a third party system - Google Drive, which enables users to create/store private keys, transfer cryptocurrencies - all without having to install any software.
Metamask
MetaMask is a bridge that allows you to visit the distributed web of tomorrow in your browser today. MetaMask includes a secure identity vault, providing a user interface to manage your identities on different sites and sign blockchain transactions
Ledger
The Ledger Hardware Wallet is a secure device that stores your private keys. Make sure all your crypto assets are safe, wherever you go

Accept and Deposit

1
Get Email Notification
When someone sends you an transfer, you will receive a notification via email indicating a transfer has been sent to you
2
Enter Security Answer
Simply click the link in the notification email, login using your Google account, and enter your security answer
3
Deposit to Wallet
With the correct security answer, you will be able to deposit the funds into the wallet of your choice

Your Questions Answered

Is Chainsfr free?
Yes. The Chainsfr service itself does not charge any service fee. However you still have to pay transaction fees on the blockchain.
How does Chainsfr work and how safe is Chainsfr?
As a financial and cryptocurrency service, we try our best to build Chainsfr as secure as possible. Chainsfr service use your Google account to store your crypto wallet and transfer information.
Do I need to register for an Chainsfr account to send or receive?
No. You only need a Google account. You can track the status of your transaction through the link in your email.
Does Chainsfr store my private keys?
No. We do not store any private keys in our database. The private keys of Drive Wallet are stored in your own Google Drive space.
Is Chainsfr open-source?
Yes. Chainsfr is open-source under the MIT License.
Do you provide support for customers?
Yes. If you have any questions don't hesitate to contact us at chat.chainsfr.com
